Our Clients Say!!!

Hot Master restaurant sells a variety of food, including beef, pork, chicken, and fish. We went out for the evening, and the venue was excellent with music playing in the background. All of the meals are freshly prepared. The costs are affordable. On-site parking is also available.

Ishanka Abeysekara

One of the best places around for mainly the dinner or an evening gathering. parking is available roadside. Eventhough the space is limited, its well managed. Everything looks so good. Speaking of food, they have a superb pasta collection and food platters. Cleanliness is well maintained, staff is friendly.

Aruna Jayanath

We had a birthday celebration here & they arranged everything without hesitance. Prices are very cheap & food is more than enough for more people. They do not add VAT & service charges for the final bill. We asked them why they're not charging VAT or service charges; & their reply was "What they care is the food quality & satisfaction only; and not profit". Perfect food quality & many variations. Will recommend 100%.
